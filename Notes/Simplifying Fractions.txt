Simplifying a fraction - is finding the greatest common factor, and dividing both the numerator and denominator by that number.

1/3 + 2/5  =  3/15 + 4/15 = 7/15

Adding a fraction - Find the least common denominator, multiply the fraction to get said denominator, add the numerator. The denominator stays the same.
